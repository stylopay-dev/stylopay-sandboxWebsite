EEA & UK IBAN

The IBAN is issued by Moorwand Limited pursuant to a license from the UK Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). Moorwand is regulated and authorised to issue electronic money by the FCA under the Reference No. 900709.

Launch an eWallet program powered by Stylopay's secure and versatile "Payment Platform Solution" branded with your Organisation's branding.
