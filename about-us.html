About StyloPay StyloPay offers their versatile payment platform as a service to co-branders and corporates, making them a one-stop shop eWallet, IBAN, prepaid card programs, wearables, mobile wallets, card perso and are all available under one platform Global issuance of multi-currency eWallet and prepaid cards backed by partnerships with multiple eMoney Providers, card issuers, processors and other payment service providers Muti-lingual customer support during on-boarding and operations All backed by unique security features and PCI DSS compliance
Who We Are
We, at StyloPay, are innovator in building mobile payment solutions
In today’s world of evolving payment systems from new kinds of cards to mobile payment technologies, our APIs for industry specific mobile wallet solutions, coupled with several value added services, address the the large gaps of markets like Hospitality, Travel, Education and existing mobile applications that as a result empower the business owners (our partners) to penetrate the untapped market and create new revenue streams.
Wallets powered by StyloPay leverage NFC, is network independent and masks card authentication details. This, at a low cost and without the accompanying hassles, enables our partners to quickly launch their own branded mobile wallet solutions.
StyloPay’s flexible business model empowers businesses such as financial institutions, issuers and wallet service providers to setup their own payment solution at a low cost. It seamlessly combines mobile payments and loyalty making it useful for retailers and consumers. Furthermore, StyloPay differentiates itself by building customized solutions for each of its micro markets such as Hospitality, Retail and Educational sectors. Travellers get easy FX and use it to travel on TFL and do small buys. Wallets powered by Stylopay leverage NFC, is network independent and are secure.
