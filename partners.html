StyloPay Payment
Solution For Retailers
Features & Benefits

The StyloPay Mobile Wallet solution for retail merchants offers them opportunity to launch their own branded wallet within a retailer's existing mobile app

With this innovative mobile wallet solution, retailers can:

  • Reduce payment processing costs
    Offer cost and operational efficiency advantages in the form of reduced fraud expenses, lower payment processing fees and faster checkout times.
  • Utilize existing POS hardware
    Our Mobile Wallet solution works with existing terminals through a software push and processes consumer transactions via secure cloud computing. With this approach, no terminal upgraders are required and consumers can use today's Smartphones
  • New revenue streams
    Promote advertising, couponing, loyalty and companion offers to consumers directly through the application
  • Enhanced Security
    Customer identifiable payment account information is not stored on the phone or provided to the POS device, so customer security is enhanced and the risk of fraud is reduced
