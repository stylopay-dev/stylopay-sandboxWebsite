Company Description

Stylopay Limited is a FinTech start-up, based in the UK, with offices in India and Singapore.

StyloPay was conceptualized as an open-loop digital payments platform in 2014. Since 2017, they have built a complete and unique cards platform, conforming to MasterCard, Visa and CUPI specifications, which include card issuance, processing, card perso services, KYC services, and transaction operations support. Stylopay is one of the few companies globally that offer a payment platform, pre-integrated with the payment ecosystem, as a service to businesses. It has implemented wearable payment and NFC mobile payment.

In addition to the technology and relationship strengths, Stylopay is empowered by a rich mix of talent. The co-founders have varied and relevant experience and are supported by experienced advisors. The technology team has deep experience and has developed the current Web portals and Mobile applications of the Card Programs.
